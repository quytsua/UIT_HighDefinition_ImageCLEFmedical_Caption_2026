## Usage in UIT_HighDefinition

The auxiliary UMLS CUI predictions used by the UIT_HighDefinition Caption Prediction pipeline were generated by running the public UIT_Worker Concept Detection notebook.

In this work, the Concept Detection training and inference code was not modified. The generated CUI predictions were used only as external auxiliary inputs. They were mapped to readable biomedical terms and inserted into the Qwen3-VL prompt as optional clinical hints. They were not treated as ground-truth labels and were not forced into the generated captions.

If the UIT_Worker team later provides an official paper citation or `CITATION.cff` file, that citation should be used in place of the temporary repository citation in [`citation.bib`](citation.bib).
